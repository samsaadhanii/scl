This compound generator generates a Sanskrit compound from an Alaukika Vigraha. A list of examples from Siddhaanta Kaumudi are provided with Alukika Vigraha. There is also an option to test your own examples. In order to test your own examples, you should provide the alaukika vigraha as well as the samāsa vidhāyaka sūtra that governs the formation of the desired samāsa.
