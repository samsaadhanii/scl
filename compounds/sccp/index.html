Introduction

   The constituency parser is third step to paraphrase. This module parses the segmented compound syntactically by pairing up the constituents in a certain order two at a time.

Sumitrā-ānanda-vardhanaḥ

is segmented as

<<Sumitrā-ānanda>-vardhanaḥ>
